What we do?

Aged homes and Orphanages
To establish and run the rehabilitation centers, Aged homes, Orphanages, Crèches, short stay homes and Day care centers for the aged and people who are in most disadvantaged
Training to children
To train childrens for democratic action and faithful leadership by introducing and effectively implementing social, educational and economic programs.
Leadership and service
To promote patriotism, leadership and service activities among youth and to foster moral values in different sections of the society with particular emphasis on the younger generations.
