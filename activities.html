Activities

Activities

To establish and run the rehabilitation centers, Aged homes, Orphanages, Crèches, short stay homes and Day care centers for the aged and people who are in most disadvantaged. To establish an alternative developmental activities for the weaker section of the society. The development of the individual, family and community through a well-integrated educational process, formal, informal or functional level. To continue to develop year by year as to promote education, such as starting educational institutions and Technical training and Research Programs based on the vision of the trust.

To promote personal, family, community, regional and general development affecting human consideration, understanding and fellowship. To train people for democratic action and faithful leadership by introducing and effectively implementing social, educational and economic programs. To promote the Self Help Groups, Community Based Organizations for their empowerment through training, Micro credit, Capacity building, as well as the total development of the society. To implement training, development and welfare projects especially for the uplift of downtrodden and other weaker sections which are socially, educationally and economically backward classes. To serve the people to relieve from the Physical / Psychological and Emotional problems as well as the disease such as T.B, Malariya, Flariya, HIV/AIDS and PLHAs etc.

To provide food, healing, shelters, training and education to undertake or arrest in work calculated directly to relieve that purpose. To promote patriotism, leadership and service activities among youth and to foster moral values in different sections of the society with particular emphasis on the younger generations. To give and grant financial assistance whether by way of contribution or loans with or without any security to any persons, bodies or agencies upon terms which will ensure that the monies are used of for the attainment of the objects and purpose of society.

To implement training, development and welfare projects especially for the uplift of Rural and Slum communities and other weaker sections which are socially, educationally and economically backward classes. To set up a rural hospital and an experimental centre for the promotion and interaction of all branches of medicine and to establish herbal gardens for raising medicinal herbs and plants. To undertake ecological and environmental studies, development of alternative energy sources and appropriate technology and also to youths and Ex-servicemen and seeking help from Government and other sources.
